Frequently Asked Questions

Before Joining

How do I know if I’m ready for A2?

You should have completed A1 or have equivalent knowledge. If you’ve done A1 elsewhere, you’ll need to take our Analysis Test to confirm readiness.

How do I take the Placement & Analysis Test?

Connect with Vyom at 514-726-5114 to schedule your test. The test costs $50 CAD, includes detailed feedback, and the amount will be credited towards your A2 program fee if you join.

Can I join if I’ve done A1 at another institute?

Yes — as long as you pass the analysis test to confirm you’re ready for A2.

Program Details

What’s included in A2 Intensive?

You get 4 live sessions per week (3 doubt-clearing + 1 activity), structured lesson plans, advanced grammar mastery, speaking assignments, real-world vocabulary, listening & reading practice tasks, writing tasks, and TEF-style exercises woven in for early exam readiness.

Is there a community group for A2 Intensive students?

Yes — you’ll be part of an active learning community for daily practice, peer feedback, and extra support between classes.

What happens if I miss a live class? Are they recorded?

Yes — all live sessions are recorded and shared in the group on request so you can catch up or review later.

Learning Process & Duration

How long will it take to complete A2 Intensive?

Most students finish in 1.5 to 2 months with 2–3 hours of daily study.

Does it take 2 months for everyone?

No — that’s the average. Some progress faster, others slower depending on schedule and consistency.

How is progress tracked?

By completing units, assignments, and participation in live sessions.

Exam Readiness

Does A2 Intensive prepare me for TEF/TCF?

Yes, the main goal is mastering A2, Exam tasks are integrated so your prep starts early.

What’s included in the A2 test?

A full evaluation in grammar, listening, reading, and speaking assignments, with personalised feedback so you know exactly what to work on before B1.

Are all assignments mandatory?

Yes — they’re carefully designed to help you master every skill. Students who complete them consistently finish B1-ready and confident.

Promotions or Offers

Do I get a discount on referring a friend?

Yes! For every friend you refer, you’ll receive $35 off!

Is there a Combo Discount?

Absolutely. We offer special combo pricing when you enroll in multiple programs together. For details, message on WhatsApp at 514-726-5114 directly.
