Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our French learning programs

Does Frenchify Program offer TEF/TCF or TEFAQ training?
Yes, Exam specific training is provided right from the start and the programs are designed in a way to fast-track the preparation.
How much time does it take to clear TEF with Frenchify?
If you study average 2-3 hours daily A1 & A2 can be completed within 3 months, B1 & B2 within 5-6 months. If you are studying French full-time the time frame reduces.
Does the Frenchify Program have doubt sessions?
Yes. Along with the online programs, we provide 4 live doubt sessions each week for better understanding and mentorship.
At what level does TEF/TCF-specific training start in Frenchify?
At B1 level, mock exam practices begin with sessions focusing on exam-specific preparation covering all 4 modules (Reading, Writing, Speaking, Listening).
